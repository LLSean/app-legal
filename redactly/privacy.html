Redactly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Redactly is built with privacy at its core. All image processing happens entirely on your device. We never see, access, or store your photos.

1. Overview

Redactly ("the App") is a privacy tool that helps you redact sensitive information from screenshots and photos. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it's used, and your rights.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Redactly does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your photos, screenshots, or any image data
  • Text detected in your images (phone numbers, emails, names, addresses, etc.)
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes

3. On-Device Processing

All image analysis — including text recognition (OCR), face detection, and sensitive data classification — is performed entirely on your device using Apple's native Vision and Natural Language frameworks. No image data is ever uploaded to any server.

The App does not require an internet connection to perform its core functions (importing, analyzing, redacting, and exporting images).

4. Subscription Data

Redactly offers optional paid subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. To process subscriptions, we use RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management platform.

RevenueCat may receive the following anonymous data from Apple:

  • An anonymous app user ID (not linked to your identity)
  • Purchase and subscription status information
  • Transaction history related to in-app purchases

This data is used solely to manage your subscription status and cannot be used to identify you personally. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

5. Photo Library & Camera Access

The App requests access to your Photo Library and Camera solely for the purpose of importing images to redact and saving redacted images. These permissions are required for the App to function but are never used to collect or transmit your data.

6. EXIF Metadata

Redactly provides an optional feature to strip EXIF metadata (including location, camera information, and timestamps) from exported images. This feature is designed to further protect your privacy when sharing photos.

7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into Redactly is RevenueCat for subscription management. We do not use any:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data
  • Social media SDKs or login providers

8. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain. Edit history (a Pro feature) is stored entirely on your device using local storage and is never transmitted externally.

9. Children's Privacy

Redactly does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require account creation or any personal information to use.

10. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations, you have rights regarding your personal data. Since Redactly does not collect personal data, these rights are inherently satisfied. If you have any concerns, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
